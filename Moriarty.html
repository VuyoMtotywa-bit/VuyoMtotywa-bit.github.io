Employee Payroll System
What it is

Advanced OOP payroll architecture using a three-class hierarchy. Abstract base defines the employee contract. Two child classes each implement salary calculation differently — same method, different logic, different result. Textbook polymorphism in a real business context.

  • ClsEmployee marked MustInherit — cannot be instantiated directly
  • ClsFullTimeEmployee — monthly pay = Annual Salary ÷ 12
  • ClsCasualEmployee — pay = Pay Rate × Hours Worked
  • Both override CalcNetSalary() — polymorphism demonstrated precisely
  • Option Strict On enforced throughout
